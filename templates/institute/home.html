NAAC

The National Assessment and Accreditation Council is a government organisation in India that assesses and accredits Higher Education Institutions. It is an autonomous body funded by the University Grants Commission and headquartered in Bangalore

About Us

NAAC data Aggregator is a Website which helps institutes to gather specific data related to Naac from all the different type of users and then collect them in the databse which can be verified by the institution administation and then the admin can download all the criteria that are required by NAAC.
